Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

KatClaw is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your Mac. We designed it this way because privacy matters.

Local-Only Architecture

KatClaw is a native macOS application — not a cloud service. There are no hosted servers, no user accounts, and no public-facing endpoints. Everything runs on your Mac:

  • KatClaw and OpenClaw run entirely on your local machine
  • All workspace files, configurations, and task logs are stored locally
  • No telemetry, analytics, or usage tracking of any kind
  • No data is shared between users — each installation is independent

What Leaves Your Mac

The only data that leaves your Mac is what you explicitly send to external services:

  • AI Providers: When tasks are processed, prompts are sent to your chosen AI provider (Claude, GPT, Gemini, etc.) via their official APIs. The provider needs input to generate responses — you choose which provider to use.
  • Telegram (optional): If you configure Telegram as a remote interface, messages pass through Telegram's servers. This is entirely optional.
  • License Validation: KatClaw contacts our licensing server to validate your purchase. We receive your license key and a hardware identifier — no personal data.

What We Collect

Anogeissus Limited ("we", "us", "our") collects minimal data:

  • Purchase Information: Email and payment details via Paddle (our payment processor). We don't store your payment card.
  • License Data: License key, activation status, and hardware identifier for license enforcement.
  • Support Tickets: If you contact support, we keep your correspondence to help you.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your task logs and automation history
  • Your workspace files or memories
  • Your API keys or credentials
  • Analytics or telemetry from the app

Third-Party Services

KatClaw integrates with services that have their own privacy policies:

  • AI Providers: Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc. — see their respective policies
  • Telegram: telegram.org/privacy
  • Paddle: Our payment processor — paddle.com/legal/privacy

Data Security

We maintain reasonable security practices:

  • All API communications use HTTPS
  • License validation uses secure endpoints
  • We don't store data we don't need

Your Rights

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you by contacting support. Since most data stays on your Mac, you control it directly.
